Our Story

Arti Metal

The legacy got the spark from Job person to an Industry Leader, from an idea to acutally implementing and scaling the idea on a large scale, from nothing to something worth mentioning and here that spark Still prevails in the heart of every person here at ArtiMetal. Experience of 30+ years, the CEO, Mr. Pramod Kumar Jain, has the vision to be a leading player in Aluminium and Copper Industry and vision of providing High-quality Metal Casting Solutions. The passion to learn, improve and face obstacles, the curiosity to know more and enhance the product quality makes us unique.

We constantly keep an eye on technology, production process, minimizing wastage and most importantly to maintain quality, to remain competitive through the years. Our CEO has started ARTI METAL to cater varied industries with our products. Our previous companies include Jain castings (1998-2003), Arti Metals(2002-2014), Roop Metals Pvt. Ltd. (est. 2011). We feel proud for successfully serving the industries since last 2+ decades and look forward to keep evolving in our field. With current combined production capacity of 600 MT per year, we continue to grow with proper Quality monitoring and best Customer Experience.
